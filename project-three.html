Research Proposal Figma Presentation

This Figma presentation introduces “A Proposed Inventory and Sales Tracking System for Rebleza Computer Parts & Accessories Shop.” It highlights the team’s research on the client’s current business operations, identifying challenges in manual tracking, stock monitoring, and sales documentation. The presentation outlines a streamlined workflow solution designed to automate inventory updates, reduce human error, and improve sales accuracy. With a clean and modern layout, the design showcases how the proposed system can enhance efficiency, provide real-time data visibility, and support smoother day-to-day management for the client’s growing business.
