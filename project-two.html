"THE FILIPINO'S GUIDE TO DATA BREACH SURVIVAL Infographic Website"

This brochure, “The Filipino’s Guide to Data Breach Survival,” provides a quick and practical guide on what to do when personal information is compromised. It outlines essential steps such as recognizing warning signs, securing accounts, reporting incidents to the National Privacy Commission, and monitoring for identity threats. Designed for everyday users, it delivers easy-to-follow tips to help Filipinos stay safe, informed, and protected in the digital world.
