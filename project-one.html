Group 1’s infographic presents a forward-looking concept titled “Smart Numbers: The Future of Calculation (2030)”, showcasing how AI transforms calculators into intelligent financial assistants. The design blends a futuristic digital theme with clear, organized text sections that explain key features such as natural language interfaces, predictive analytics, and real-world budgeting applications. Overall, the presentation effectively communicates how everyday financial tasks could become more intuitive, personalized, and AI-powered by 2030.
